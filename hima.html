About Us

OrphanCare is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children. Founded in 2010, we've helped over 5,000 children find loving homes and provided education, healthcare, and emotional support to thousands more.

Our mission is to ensure that every child grows up in a safe, nurturing environment with access to education and opportunities to thrive.

Our Programs

Foster Care

Connecting children with loving foster families while seeking permanent adoption solutions.

Education

Providing quality education, school supplies, and scholarships for orphaned children.

Healthcare

Ensuring access to medical care, nutrition, and mental health support.

Counseling

Professional counseling services to help children heal from trauma and loss.
